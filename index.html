Cornerstones

Mission

Our brand aims to bring expats out of their comfort zone and homesickness by connecting them to various events, gatherings or parties within their cities or neighboring cities.

Vision

In a world where a new environment, a new culture and new people can be a scary experience. We find it important for people to find a community where they can feel a sense of belonging and connect with various people within or outside their culture to ease their homesickness so that they can feel Home Away from home.

Target Group

We aim to reach eager expats in the Netherlands between the age of 20 - 35 years old, who are coming from non-EU countries.
